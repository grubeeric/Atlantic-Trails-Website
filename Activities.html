Activities at Atlantic Trails

Hiking

Atlantic Trails Resort has 5 miles of hiking trails and is adjacent to a state park. Go at it alone or join one of our guided hikes. The table below lists some of our guided hikes and their departure times from the main office. Sign-up in the office for any or all of the hikes during your stay at Atlantic Trails. Our guides will point out many interesting features and landmarks during you hike.

Tour Start Time Description Guide Time
6:00 am 2 mile hike on the upper ridge - watch the sun rise. Stephanie 2 hours
8:00 am 4 mile hike in the state park. Transportation leaves from the main office. Roger 3 hours
1:00 pm 6 mile hike over the full trail and the beach head. Some light climbing. Stephanie or Heidi 4.5 hours

Kayaking

Ocean kayaks are available for guest use. The table provides a summary of out kayaks. Be sure of your kayaking skills. Check the links to identify your skill level. If you have any questions, please contact Dave in the main office.

Kayak Type Description Skill Level
Sea Long, flat bottom, cargo area for your personal effects. Low to moderate.
Surf Shorter, more manuverable than sea kayak. Moderate to high
White Water Very short, rounded hull, very robust. Highly manuverable. Expert

Bird Watching

While anytime is a good time for bird watching at Atlantic Trails, we offer guided birdwatching trips at sunrise several times a week. The table below lists some of these trips. See the main office for more details.

Tour Start Time Description Commonly Seen Birds Guide
5:30 am Tuesdays Only- September thru November 1.5 mile hike on the upper ridge. Lasts about 1 hour Green Herons, Northern Harriers, Ospreys Stephanie
6:00 am Saturday and Sunday Only - March thru June 3 mile hike on the beach. Lasts about 2 hours Cormorants, Canadian Geese, Least Sandpiper Heidi
5:30 am Thursdays Only - June thru August 3 mile hike in the State Park. Some light climbing. Lesser Yellowlegs, Willet, Black-bellied Plover Stephanie or Heidi
